# Primitive: Natural numbers

The [natural numbers](Data.Nat.html) are the h-initial type generated by
a point and an endomorphism.

```agda
open import Agda.Builtin.Nat public
  using (Nat; suc; zero)
  renaming (_+_ to infixl 6 _+_
           ;_-_ to infixl 6 _-_
           ;_*_ to infixl 7 _*_
           ;_==_ to infix  4 _≡ᵇ_
           ;_<_ to infix  4 _<ᵇ_
           )
```
